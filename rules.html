It's A Game


Cross the finishline first! Yup, it's that simple.

Roll the Die

Hit the play button to determine which player goes first. After that choose the die you want to roll. The number rolled will show up in the center display and your character will move those spaces.

Reverse #s

Roll strategically, because some numbers send you backwards.

  • 20 Sided #s: 17, 16, 15, 14
  • 12 Sided #s: 9, 8, 7, 6
  • 8 Sided #s: 5, 4
  • 6 Sided #s: 3

Electric Shock

Earn an "Electric Shock" by either rolling a 10 or 13. Then on any turn after that you may use it to send your opponent back 30 spaces. Choose wisely when to use this power. Once you use it, you lose it.
